Problem Statement

A lot of students find it hard to find good but free revision materials
for the specific subjects/topics they need. The resources that are already
available are often scattered across multiple websites, are low quality
or locked behind paywalls.

This makes studying harder than it has to be especially for students
who dont want to pay for resources.

Stakeholders

  • Students

    Students are the primary users of the platform, benefiting from all its features.

  • Teachers

    Teachers help maintain the quality and reliability of the platform by:

    • Uploading accurate, high-quality resources.
    • Removing low-quality or inappropriate materials.
    • This ensures that students have access to the best educational content.
